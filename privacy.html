Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

BawTV (“the app”) is a personal-use Roku channel that plays live TV from a playlist the user configures. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information we collect

BawTV does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. We do not require an account, we do not use third-party analytics or advertising, and we do not track your viewing activity off the device.

Data stored on your device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your Roku to make it work — for example, your saved favorites and the playlist URL you provide. This information stays on the device and is not sent to us. Removing the channel removes this data.

Network requests

To show channels and play video, the app contacts the playlist and streaming servers specified by the playlist you configure. The app does not send your data to any server operated by BawTV.

Children’s privacy

BawTV is intended for personal use by adults and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
