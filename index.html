BawTV
GEAUX LIVE

BawTV is a personal streaming app for Roku that plays live TV channels from a playlist the user configures. It’s a simple, ad-free way to browse your channels by category, search across them, save favorites, and watch — right from your TV.

Features
  • Browse channels by category
  • Search across all channels
  • Save and quickly reach favorites
  • Now / next program info where available

About

BawTV is an independent, personal-use Roku channel. It is not affiliated with Roku, Inc. or with any content provider. BawTV does not host, provide, or sell any video content — it simply plays media from a playlist the user supplies.
